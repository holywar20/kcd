session-log-never — Habit

Why

any point a session would otherwise fire session-log-aggressive — a user "log" request, or session close after a change to _Claude/

Action

do nothing; write no line to _Claude/logs/session.md, regardless of the trigger

Explanation

Some sessions are not part of the project's real history — a disposable test-harness run, an ephemeral experiment, a throwaway probe — and a breadcrumb for one of those is noise a future session would waste time orienting against. This habit declares that intent explicitly (by injected context, a Constellation node, or a dedicated lens) rather than relying on everyone remembering to skip the step by hand.
