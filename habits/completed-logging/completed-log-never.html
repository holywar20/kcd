completed-log-never — Habit

Why

any point a session would otherwise fire completed-log-aggressive — substantial lens work that would normally earn a detailed entry

Action

do nothing; write no entry to any lens's completed log, regardless of how substantial the work was

Explanation

Pairs with session-log-never for sessions that aren't part of the project's real history, but can also apply independently — a session that wants the terse breadcrumb (orientation) without the detailed completed-log overhead (a quick fix, a one-off investigation).
