append-completed-entry — Habit

When

At session close, for each lens this session advanced.

Action

Prepend one entry to the lens's completed log at its completed_path (_Claude/logs/{lensname}/completed/). Capture what was done, why, and what it unlocks.

Format: - [YYYY-MM-DD] {what, why, what it unlocks}

Thin (avoid): - [2026-05-09] Updated frontend lens.

Rich (target): - [2026-05-09] Frontend: encoded accordion rebuild the old expansion panels, unblocks the detail left-panel work.
