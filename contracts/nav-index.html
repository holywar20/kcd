contracts — Nav index

Human-in-the-loop session lifecycles. A contract describes how a human-in-session and Claude collaborate on a recurring kind of work — plan authoring, lens deployment, and so on. Contracts differ from habits (atomic single-step reflexes): a contract is a multi-phase lifecycle with a human in the loop.

A contract's scope declares where it is wired: universal (referenced from _lens_base, loaded every session) or lens:{name} (referenced from a single lens's Know block).
