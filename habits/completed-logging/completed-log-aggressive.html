completed-log-aggressive — Habit

Why

substantial lens work — the session meaningfully advanced a lens's actual output, not a trivial or exploratory touch

Action

prepend one entry to the active lens's completed log at its completed path (_Claude/logs/{lensname}/completed/), format - [YYYY-MM-DD] {what, why, what it unlocks}, for each lens the session advanced

Explanation

Companion to session-log-aggressive's breadcrumb: the breadcrumb always fires on request, but the completed entry only earns its place when the work is substantial enough to be worth the detail — what was done, why, and what it unlocks, as much as is worth keeping. Thin (avoid): - [2026-05-09] Updated frontend lens. Rich (target): - [2026-05-09] Frontend: encoded accordion rebuilt over the old expansion panels — unblocks the detail left-panel work.
