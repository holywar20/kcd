Habit — add-todo

When: a session surfaces an open design decision, a deferred item, or a pending dependency worth tracking.

Action: append a dated entry - [ ] [YYYY-MM-DD] {item} to the active lens's todo list at its todo_path (_Claude/logs/{lensname}/todo/). Group under a sub-header if the lens already organizes todos by topic.

Todos live at the lens's todo_path, not in the lens body — agents know where they are but do not read them unless asked.
