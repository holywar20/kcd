subagents-allowed — Habit

When

a task could be decomposed or parallelized and a subagent would offload it

Action

spawn a subagent for delegatable work without waiting for the user to ask by name

Explanation

The opposite pole of no-subagents — exists so a lens or session that genuinely wants autonomous delegation can opt in explicitly rather than fighting the default. Off by default everywhere today: a real, unsettled tradeoff (cold-start cost, lost lens context) worth experimenting with rather than enabling broadly yet.
