subagents-allowed — Habit

When

A task could be decomposed or parallelized and a subagent would offload it.

Action

Spawn a subagent for delegatable work without waiting for the user to ask by name.

The opposite pole of no-subagents — exists so a lens or session that genuinely wants autonomous delegation can opt in explicitly, rather than fighting the default. Off by default everywhere today: this is a real, unsettled tradeoff (cold-start cost, lost lens context) worth experimenting with rather than one to enable broadly yet.
