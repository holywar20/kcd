append-completed-entry — Habit

When

at session close, for each lens this session advanced

Action

prepend one entry to the lens's completed log at its completed_path (_Claude/logs/{lensname}/completed/), format - [YYYY-MM-DD] {what, why, what it unlocks}

Explanation

Capture what was done, why, and what it unlocks — rich, not thin. Thin (avoid): - [2026-05-09] Updated frontend lens. Rich (target): - [2026-05-09] Frontend: encoded accordion rebuild — the old expansion panels, unblocks the detail left-panel work. The completed log carries the weight the terse session breadcrumb only points at.
